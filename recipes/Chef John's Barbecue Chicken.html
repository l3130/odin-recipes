Chef John's Barbecue Chicken

Description

This recipe for Chef John's Barbecue Chicken features tender, juicy chicken coated in a homemade barbecue sauce that is both tangy and sweet. Perfect for summer cookouts or any time you crave delicious barbecue!

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs, bone-in and skin-on
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Rub the chicken thighs with olive oil, then season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken on the grill, skin side down, and cook for about 5-7 minutes until the skin is crispy.
  4. Flip the chicken over and brush generously with barbecue sauce. Cook for another 10-15 minutes, basting with more sauce halfway through.
  5. Ensure the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165°F (75°C).
  6. Remove from the grill and let rest for a few minutes before serving.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired and serve with extra barbecue sauce on the side.
