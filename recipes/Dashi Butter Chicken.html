Dashi Butter Chicken

Description

This Dashi Butter Chicken is a fusion dish that combines the rich flavors of traditional butter chicken with the umami depth of dashi broth, creating a unique and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1 cup dashi broth
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice or naan for serving

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until translucent.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, cooking for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the curry powder and cook for 30 seconds to release its flavors.
  4. Add the chicken thighs to the skillet, cooking until they are browned on all sides.
  5. Pour in the dashi broth and crushed tomatoes, bringing the mixture to a simmer.
  6. Reduce heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Stir in the heavy cream, adjusting seasoning with salt and pepper as needed. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  8. Serve hot over cooked rice or with naan, garnished with fresh cilantro.
